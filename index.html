Movie Reccomendation System

EDA, MACHINE LEARNING

The given two datasets which are available from Kaggle provide us with information about the movie and cast over the past few years. The original content-based recommender system is the continuation and development of collaborative filtering, which doesn’t need the user’s evaluation for items. Instead, the similarity is calculated based on the information of items that are chosen by users and then make the recommendation accordingly. We can implement machine learning model algorithms that use cosine similarity in the backend to recommend movies to customers.

Car Price Prediction Analyser

EDA, MACHINE LEARNING

The aforementioned dataset was made accessible to us through Kaggle, and it supplied us with a variety of information regarding automobiles over time. The data set required a great deal of organizing and cleaning up before it could be used. The overarching goal of the project is to provide a centralized location from where users will be able to search for the make and model of a vehicle they are interested in purchasing and compare the pricing offered by each of the several online marketplaces where the vehicle is sold.

Covid-19 Data Analysis

DATA ANALYTICS, EDA

Our World In Data is where we got our hands on this massive dataset. Information such as daily case counts, death tolls, immunisation rates, and so forth were recorded. Our SQL queries yielded useful insights, and we utilised Tableau to visualise the results. The project's goal was to build a dashboard that provided an overview of the growing covid-19 cases and fatalities throughout the world and made it possible to comprehend a vast quantity of data with the use of visualization tools.
